Who is Crazy Monkey Garage SA

Crazy Monkey Garage (CMG) SA was founded in 2018 by Mr. Jaco Thompson and has since become a name well known for its great service and affordable prices.

At CMG SA every client receives service personally from Jaco himself, making each client feel like they a part of the family. This sets CMG SA apart from major competitors and gives a welcome atmosphere to the entire business.

Working with CMG SA you will not only find great service that is centrally located, personal and professional service, fast lead times that are cost-effective and that are competitive, years of service within the industry, and CMG SA only sources quality products from well established and well-known vendors.

CMG SA offers various services from major to minor vehicle services of vehicles, to lift kits, and even project vehicles. For a more extensive list of our services please refer to our services section.
